# Conversion Strategy

## Primary Conversion

Primary action:

`Book a Private Consultation`

The website should repeatedly but calmly guide qualified visitors toward a private consultation request.

## Secondary Actions

- Call Jolie
- Get Directions
- Ask a Question

## Conversion Principles

- Make the consultation path obvious on every major page.
- Keep the form short enough to complete on mobile.
- Reassure visitors that they do not need to know the exact solution before reaching out.
- Avoid competing CTAs that dilute the consultation goal.
- Use phone, email, and directions as persistent fallback actions.

## Recommended Consultation Form Fields

Need confirmation before final implementation:

- Name
- Phone
- Email
- What can Jolie help with?
- Preferred contact method
- Optional message

Do not ask for sensitive medical details unless explicitly approved and supported by appropriate privacy handling.

## Measurement Needs

Decide before launch:

- Where form submissions should be delivered
- Whether phone taps should be tracked
- Whether directions clicks should be tracked
- Analytics provider
- Consent or privacy requirements
