# Content Plan

## Public Messaging Priorities

The website should make these ideas clear:

- Wigs by Jolie is a boutique premium wig and hair-solutions studio.
- Jolie provides private, personalized guidance.
- Clients do not need to know which hair solution they need before reaching out.
- Services include wigs, toppers, extensions, styling, and care.
- The goal is a natural-looking, comfortable, personally selected solution.
- The next step is to book a private consultation, call, get directions, or ask a question.

## Approved CTA Language

Primary:

- Book a Private Consultation

Secondary:

- Call Jolie
- Get Directions
- Ask a Question

## Required Public Information

- Business name
- Address
- Phone
- Email
- Service overview
- Consultation-focused next step
- Location context for Deerfield Beach

## Do Not Publish Until Confirmed

- Hours
- Pricing
- Appointment duration
- Consultation fee or free consultation wording
- Policies
- Testimonials
- Review counts
- Certifications
- Awards
- Insurance or financing details
- Claims about specific product inventory

## Draft Page Content Needs

- Homepage hero and value proposition
- Consultation process explanation
- Service overview copy
- First-time buyer reassurance
- Location and contact section
- FAQ copy based only on confirmed facts
- Form field labels and success/error states
