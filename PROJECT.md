# Wigs by Jolie Project Foundation

## Project Goal

Create a premium, mobile-first, consultation-focused website for Wigs by Jolie, a boutique wig and hair-solutions studio in Deerfield Beach, Florida.

The primary business goal is to generate qualified private consultation requests.

## Customer Outcome

Visitors should understand that Jolie helps clients find a hair solution that:

- Looks natural
- Feels comfortable
- Complements their face and personal style
- Fits their daily routine
- Provides appropriate coverage or volume
- Feels personally selected rather than taken from a shelf

Visitors should not need to know whether they need a wig, topper, extensions, or another solution before requesting a consultation.

## Scope

Approved direction:

- Consultation-focused website
- Premium editorial visual system
- Local service business SEO
- Contact, phone, email, directions, and consultation request paths
- Educational content that reduces first-time buyer uncertainty

Out of scope unless explicitly approved:

- Ecommerce
- Financing
- Insurance workflows
- AI matching
- Face scanning
- Home trials
- Subscriptions
- Virtual consultations
- Large online catalog
