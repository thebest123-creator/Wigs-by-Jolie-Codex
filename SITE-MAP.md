# Site Map

## Recommended Initial Site

Start with a focused small site rather than a large catalog.

Recommended pages:

- Home
- Services
- Consultation
- About Jolie
- Contact

Optional later pages:

- Care and Maintenance
- First-Time Wig Guide
- Toppers and Coverage Guide
- Blog or Articles

## Homepage Sections

- Editorial hero with primary consultation CTA
- Who Jolie helps
- Service pathways
- Private consultation explanation
- Natural-looking, comfortable, personalized outcome messaging
- About Jolie preview
- Location and contact block
- Final consultation CTA

## Services Page Sections

- Service overview
- Wigs and fittings
- Toppers and coverage
- Extensions and volume
- Styling, maintenance, and care
- Consultation CTA

## Consultation Page Sections

- What the consultation helps clarify
- What visitors do and do not need to know beforehand
- Inquiry form
- Phone and directions fallback

## Contact Page Sections

- Address
- Phone
- Email
- Directions link
- Inquiry form
- Map embed, if approved

## Navigation

Primary navigation should stay short:

- Services
- Consultation
- About
- Contact

Primary nav CTA:

- Book a Private Consultation
