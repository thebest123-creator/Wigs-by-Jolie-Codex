## Photography Guidance

Best assets for this site:

- Authentic studio imagery
- Product detail closeups
- Editorial hair movement and texture
- Private consultation environment
- Warm, high-quality portrait photography

Avoid:

- Generic stock salon photos
- Stock images presented as real clients
- Low-resolution social screenshots
- Images with unconfirmed usage rights

## File Organization

Use:

- `assets/brand/`
- `assets/photography/`
- `assets/inspiration/`
- `assets/originals/`

Keep original source files in `assets/originals/` and edited/exported versions in the appropriate working folder.
