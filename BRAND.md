# Brand Direction

## Brand Position

Wigs by Jolie should be presented as a premium, private, boutique hair-solutions studio for clients who want personal guidance, natural-looking results, and a calm consultation experience.

## Brand Attributes

- Premium
- Editorial
- Private
- Warm
- Calm
- Reassuring
- Fashion-forward
- Personal
- Trustworthy
- Established

## Voice

Use language that is confident, calm, and personal. Avoid clinical fear-based copy, discount retail language, generic beauty cliches, or exaggerated transformation claims.

Good copy direction:

- "A private consultation for finding the right hair solution."
- "Guidance for wigs, toppers, extensions, styling, and care."
- "You do not need to know what you need before you come in."

Avoid:

- "Guaranteed transformation"
- "Best in Florida"
- "Free consultation"
- "Instant results"
- "Medical-grade solution"
- "Thousands of happy clients"

## Visual Direction

Prioritize:

- Real studio or product photography
- Editorial close crops
- Natural texture and movement
- Refined typography
- Warm contrast and restrained color
- Generous spacing
- Varied layouts

Avoid:

- Stock images presented as real clients
- Repetitive cards
- Generic icons over every section
- Excessive beige-on-beige styling
- Overly rounded UI
- Tech startup gradients
- Medical clinic styling
