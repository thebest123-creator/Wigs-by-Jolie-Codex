# Open Questions

Resolve these before development or before public launch.

## Business Details

- What are the current business hours?
- Is the consultation appointment-only?
- Is there a consultation fee?
- If consultations are free, can that be stated publicly?
- What is the preferred booking method?
- Should the current Wix booking flow remain active?
- Why does the Book Online page say "Nothing to book right now" while the home page shows scheduling UI?
- Should form submissions go to wigsbyjolie@gmail.com or another inbox?
- Should visitors be able to text or only call/email?

## Services And Policies

- Which services should be emphasized first?
- Are there specific brands, product lines, or materials that can be named?
- Are prices or starting ranges approved for publication?
- Are refunds, exchanges, deposits, cancellations, or care policies approved for publication?
- Is financing available?
- Is insurance accepted or assisted in any way?
- Are virtual consultations offered?

## Proof And Trust

- Are testimonials approved for use?
- Are review counts or ratings approved and sourceable?
- Which exact Google review excerpts are approved for on-site display?
- Are certifications, awards, or credentials approved and sourceable?
- Are client transformation photos approved with written permission?
- Can "family-owned" or similar ownership language be used?

## Brand And Assets

- Is there an official logo?
- Is there a brand color palette?
- Are there preferred fonts?
- Which photos are approved for website use?
- Which social channels are active and should be linked?
- What are the exact active URLs for Facebook, Instagram, YouTube, X, LinkedIn, and TikTok, if any?

## Technical

- What domain will the finished site use?
- Where will the site be hosted?
- Is the current Wix site being replaced or supplemented?
- Should the existing blog be migrated, rewritten, redirected, or left on Wix?
- Is there an existing analytics account?
- Is there an existing Google Business Profile to connect or reference?
- Are there privacy policy or terms pages available?
