# Customer Foundation

## Primary Visitors

Likely visitors include people exploring wigs, toppers, extensions, or coverage solutions for style, thinning hair, fine hair, hair loss, daily wear, or special needs. The site should not assume a single reason for visiting.

## Visitor State

Many visitors may arrive with uncertainty:

- They may not know whether they need a wig, topper, extensions, or styling support.
- They may worry whether the result will look natural.
- They may want privacy and a calm experience.
- They may need reassurance without being pushed into a medical or sales-heavy framing.

## Key Questions To Answer

- Can Jolie help with my type of hair concern?
- What kinds of solutions are available?
- Will I be guided privately?
- Do I need to know what to ask for?
- What happens during a consultation?
- How do I book, call, ask a question, or get directions?

## Content Approach

The site should guide visitors toward a private consultation rather than forcing them to self-diagnose. Service pages and sections should describe options clearly, then return to the consultation path.
