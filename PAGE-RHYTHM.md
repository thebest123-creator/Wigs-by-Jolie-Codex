# Page Rhythm

## Homepage Rhythm

1. Editorial hero: split text and large portrait/process placeholder, dark-on-light, high visual impact.
2. Guided entry: compact dark band with "not sure yet" messaging and three quiet options.
3. Outcome section: asymmetric image/text layout around natural appearance, comfort, routine, and coverage.
4. Services: four service pathways with varied sizing, not equal cards.
5. Consultation process: practical step sequence with a form-adjacent CTA.
6. About Jolie: intimate founder/studio section with portrait placeholder.
7. Gallery: mixed-ratio placeholder mosaic for future studio/product imagery.
8. FAQ: plain accessible accordion, no proof claims.
9. Location/contact: direct phone, email, directions, and consultation path.

## Consultation Page Rhythm

1. Calm intro with privacy and uncertainty reassurance.
2. Two-step consultation form.
3. Alternative phone and directions actions.
4. Short explanation that requests are not confirmed appointments until Wigs by Jolie responds.

## Privacy And Terms Rhythm

Keep these pages simple, readable, and honest. Avoid legal overreach. State that final legal review is still required before launch.
